🎯 Ideal Customer Profile

Revenue: $3M+ annual revenue
Team Size: 10+ employees
Decision Makers: Owner, CEO, President, Visionary, Integrator, Sales/Marketing Leader
Industries: Construction, Home Improvement, Manufacturing, Business Services, Retail, Professional Services
Mindset: Believes in coaching/training, open to doing things differently, research-driven buyers

❌ Disqualifiers

Solopreneur or freelancer with no team
Very early stage with no sales team
Wants "do it for you" agency, not coaching
Not in US or Canada
Marketing agency, web design, video production (we compete)
Happy with current agency, no interest in change

🗣️ Opening Questions

  • "What brought you over to our booth? What's catching your eye?"
  • "Do you currently run on EOS? How's that working for you?"
  • "EOS runs your business — what runs your growth?"
  • "Do you have a system in place to run your sales and marketing?"

🔍 Qualifying Questions

  • "What does your sales and marketing look like right now?"
  • "How are your customers finding you today?"
  • "How are people finding you — is it mostly referrals?"
  • "What are you currently spending on marketing?" 💡 Key signal
  • "How many employees do you have?" 💡 Required for booking
  • "Is sales and marketing aligned, or do they operate in separate worlds?"
  • "Have you started thinking about how AI will affect your sales and marketing?"
  • "Is this something your company is actively trying to solve, or more of a 'someday' thing?"

💬 Bob Session Follow-ups

  • "What resonated with you from Bob's session?"
  • "What are some things in your business that Bob's session would solve?"
  • "If you missed the session, I can get you those resources..."

✅ Green Light Signals - LEAN IN

"We're not getting enough leads" or "leads are inconsistent"
"Marketing and sales aren't really working together"
"We've been relying on referrals and it's unreliable/not scaling"
"We have a marketing person but don't know what's working"
"We hired an agency but we're not seeing ROI"
"We want to build marketing in-house but don't know where to start"
"AI is moving fast and we're not ready for it"
"Our traffic is down and we need to show up in AI search results"
"We want to be the go-to name in our market"

🛑 Red Flag Signals - EXIT GRACEFULLY

Capture info via lead form, give minibook, direct to endlesscustomers.com

Solo/freelancer with no employees
Very early stage, no sales team yet
Want agency to do everything, not interested in coaching
Love their current agency, seeing great results
Competing industry (marketing/web/video agencies)
